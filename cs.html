Smart Alarm Clock

The purpose of the Smart Alarm Project is to create an interactive alarm clock that requires the user to physically do an action/pose that will turn the alarm off (ie. jumping jacks, yoga sequences, specific dance moves etc.) In order to do this we used a pressure sensitive conductive sheet called velostat. When the alarm goes off the velostat squares light up and once the user puts pressure on them for at least 5 seconds the alarm will turn off.
